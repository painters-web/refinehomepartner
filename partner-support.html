Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get more leads?
Stay active during peak hours (9 AM - 6 PM) and maintain a high rating to get priority leads. Complete your KYC verification to start receiving leads immediately.
When will I get paid?
Payments are processed every Friday for all completed jobs. You can withdraw your earnings instantly once you have minimum ₹100 in your wallet.
How to improve my rating?
Complete jobs on time, communicate professionally with customers, maintain cleanliness, and ask happy customers for 5-star reviews after job completion.
What if customer cancels the job?
If a customer cancels after you reach the site, you'll receive a cancellation fee of ₹100. If cancelled before you start traveling, no fee is applicable.
How do I update my documents?
Go to Profile → KYC Verification section. You can upload your Aadhar and PAN card there. Verification usually takes 24-48 hours.
